Research

My primary research areas are:

  • Astrophysics: Using multi-messenger observations to study compact astrophysical objects and phenomena, including gravitational-wave sources.
  • Nuclear Astrophysics: Constraining the dense-matter equation of state and examining its impact on the observational signatures of neutron stars.

  • R. Kumar, V. Dexheimer, J. Jahan, J. Noronha, J. Noronha-Hostler, C. Ratti, N. Yunes, A. R. Nava Acuna, M. Alford, M. H. Anik, et al., “Theoretical and Experimental Constraints for the Equation of State of Dense and Hot Matter,” Living Reviews in Relativity 27, 3 (2024) .
