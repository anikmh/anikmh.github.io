Bio

I am a graduate student at the Department of Physics and Astronomy at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville, working as a research assistant with Dr. William R. Hix in theoretical nuclear astrophysics.

I completed my undergraduate degree in Physics at the University of Dhaka, Bangladesh in 2015. After that, I moved to the United States for graduate studies and obtained two master's degrees at Ohio University: an M.S. in Physics (2018) and an M.S. in Mathematics (2020). I joined UTK in 2020 and began my doctoral research in 2021.
